Coulture Magazine Rebrand

Our 2018 rebrand completely reimagined our brand into a more bold, recognizable, and strong presence on campus, in print, and on social media that reflects more clearly our mission of inclusivity, diversity and superior design.

Keeping print and digital platforms in mind, our intentional black and white design encompasses every theme the magazine embodies with each issue, while its boldness offers strong brand extentions both in print issues and online.
