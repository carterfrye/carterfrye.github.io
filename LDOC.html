LDOC Identity System for UNC Chapel Hill

The Carolina Union reaches out to sponsors on and off UNC’s campus to provide merchandise, free food and festivities for students on their “Last Day of Classes.” T-shirts and stickers feature the LDOC identity along with any sponsorship and administrative materials.

The union asked for an identity that would be able to evolve as the LDOC themes mold and change over the years. I designed a badge that would fit well with ever-changing color schemes and still convey a timeless sense of lightheartedness for our beloved LDOC.
